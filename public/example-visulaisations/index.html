Example Visulaisations

Here are some examples of the visualisations I made -

  1. China - Male vs Female Population - This is a visualisations I made in R using the highcharter library. There is also a play button at the bottom to view the population growth over-time.

  2. India - Domestic Violence Quiz - A visualisation to understand the physical abuse that women in India are subjected to.

  3. India - Literacy Rate - This is a report I made to visualise corealltions of Literacy Rate in different regions of the country to different variables measuresd in the 15th Indian Census (2011)

  4. Gallup Survey - India Visualisation - A visualisation of the Gallup survey data in India.

  5. India Survey PCA Report - A report that analyses the correlates of Literacy Rate using Principal Component Analysis. The report explains the different dimensions created after PCA and then clusters the districts based on different dimensions
